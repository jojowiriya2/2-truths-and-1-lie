Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 28, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how "2 Truths and 1 Lie" (the "App") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our application within Miro boards.

2. Developer Information

Developer: Wiriya Thongyut
Contact Email: jowry6@gmail.com

3. Information We Collect

The App collects and processes the following information:

  • User Identity: Your Miro user ID and username (provided by Miro SDK)
  • Game Data: Player names, statements submitted during gameplay, and voting results
  • Board Storage: Game state data stored temporarily in your Miro board's storage

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Enable multiplayer gameplay functionality
  • Synchronize game state across all players on the board
  • Display player names, statements, votes, and scores during the game

5. Data Storage and Retention

  • All game data is stored locally in your Miro board's storage using Miro's Storage Collection API
  • We do not store any data on external servers or databases
  • Game data persists only within the specific Miro board where the game is played
  • Data is deleted when you reset the game or clear your board storage
  • We do not retain any personal information beyond the active game session

6. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your personal information to third parties. All data remains within your Miro board and is visible only to users who have access to that specific board.

7. Miro Integration

This App operates within the Miro platform and uses the Miro Web SDK. By using this App, you are also subject to Miro's Privacy Policy.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the game data stored in your Miro board
  • Delete game data by resetting the game or removing the app from your board
  • Stop using the App at any time

9. Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. All data transmission occurs over HTTPS. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

10. Children's Privacy

This App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
